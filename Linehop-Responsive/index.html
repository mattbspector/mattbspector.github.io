About Us

LineHop is about helping others make more informed nightlife decisions. One question people always ask before they go out is “how long is the line?” LineHop is going to answer that question by building a community of users willing to share information to help everyone make better decisions and outsmart long lines.

How it works

Wanna know how long the line is before you leave the pregame? We’ve got you covered. The beauty of LineHop is that we are community of people looking to help each other outsmart long lines. As soon as you open the app, you’ll see a feed of all your favorite bars and everything that all of our other LineHop users have reported. LineHop gives you real time updates so you’ll always know how to plan your night.
When you’re waiting in line at your favorite bar, take a picture of it (we know you’re on your phone anyway). Let us know how long the line is, if it’s moving, or any other helpful information for the community. The more you report, the more you get rewarded. Every picture you take earns you loyalty points toward a free skip-the-line pass! Sounds like a win win to us.
Not liking how long the line is? No problem. With the ability to purchase skip-the-line passes directly from the app, you can bypass the line all together and cut right to the front. No waiting, no hassle. Don’t wait in line. LineHop.
